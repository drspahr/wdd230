ABOUT US

History

Twenty years ago, we started out as an organic fruit grower. We supplied several local grocery chains and restaurants. We were known for high quality excellant tasting products. Over the years we built greenhouses to expand our offerings. Ten years ago we took a chance and purchased our first food truck. This was an experiement to see if the general public would be interested in customer specified mixed fruit drinks.

Today

Today we operate thirty trucks throughout southern and central California. We continue to supply restaurants and grocers with high quality certified organic fruits. Our business has expanded to the international market as well. The future is bright and we look forward to bring you the best in organic fruit for all your eating needs.
